Data Science and Mathematics

About me and my work:

PhD. in Applied Mathematics and Scientific Programming
with a specialization in Data Science

My work in Artificial Intelligence is highly linked to my background in Mathematics. The conjunction of these two disciplines allows me to expand the use of existing tools and create new ones to solve complicated problems based on solid modeling of the same. In machine learning, this can be reflected, for example, in the correct statistical and geometric interpretation of the models. Likewise, algebra fundamentals help me develop vectorization processes to speed up the processes or facilitate their parallelization through CPUs or GPUs. Methods for solving Differential equations have given me ideas for extending predictions from highly populated areas to poorly populated ones using a grid. And I use Calculus every day to formulate custom metrics, weighted aggregations, transitions, visualizations and many other things. I use all these technologies to solve problems oriented to the practical needs of the user.
